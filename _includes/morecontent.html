About Me

I am a first year PhD student in the Harvard-MIT Health Sciences & Technology Medical Engineering and Medical Physics program. Previously, I graduated summa cum laude and with departmental distinction in biomedical engineering from Duke University.

My research interests lie at the intersection of biological engineering and translational medicine - exploring the rational design of diagnostic and therapeutic platforms for probing and solving the world's most pressing healthcare challenges.

Outside of the lab, I enjoy sailing, scuba diving, hiking, and cheering on Duke Basketball.
