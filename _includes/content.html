About Me

I am currently a first year PhD student in the Harvard-MIT Health Sciences & Technology Medical Engineering and Medical Physics Program. Previously, I graduated summa cum laude with departmental distinction in biomedical engineering from Duke University.

My interests lie at the intersection of materials science and biological engineering - exploring the rational design of biomaterials platforms for probing and treating the world's most pressing healthcare challenges.
